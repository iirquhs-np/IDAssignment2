We're Here To Help You

Singapore Customers
As shown on the prices on our website, we support Internet Banking payment, NETs payment, Credit Card payments and Paypal. Please do note that the base prices on our site are for Cash and Nets discounted prices. Credit card or paypal payments are listed on our cart page. This is to allow us to bring the best prices to our customers should they so choose.

Cheque payments and direct bank transfers are also accepted but please do note that these have to be cleared before collection or delivery will be allowed. Do note that bank transfers between different banks can take around 3 working days or more (We use DBS).

We also provide installment plan services for our customers who require this. The installment plans provided are by DBS Bank, and thus only DBS / POSB Credit Cards (not Debit Cards) will be able to make use of these installment plans. The options offered are 6 month and 12 month plans, with a corresponding additional admin charge of 3.5% and 4% respectively. Installment plans are only avaliable at our showroom.

Overseas Customers
For overseas customers, we support telegraphic transfers and paypal payments. As per above, do note that paypal transfers will be subject to an additional 3% admin fee charge which is paid directly to paypal. For both, do note that for both stages (system building / delivery), actions will only commence after we have received the payment.

Do note that COMZONE does not currently provide overseas shipping for warranty. Our warranty coverage is local only to countries where a service center is present, and logistics costs of overseas shipping for warranty is not borne by COMZONE.

COMZONE provides free life time service support for original purchasers of our systems. If your warranty has expired, you may call or email our support staff for assistance. Please note that price for parts will still be charged if your parts warranty has expired.

COMZONE will support any upgrading of systems provided we still carry the parts / the parts are technically compatible. No Service Charge will be charged to our customers for any upgrade services. Please contact our sales team for any upgrade requests.

COMZONE does not buy back used components.

All COMZONE systems shipped in Singapore are assembled by our local production team based in Singapore, while units shipped in other parts of the world are assembled in Australia by a local team based in Australia.

Our COMZONE standard package includes the following:
- Laptop / Desktop PC
- AC Power Adapter (For Laptops)
- Power Cord
- LI-ION Battery (For Laptops)
- User Manual
- Drivers CD (For Laptops)

COMZONE is based in Singapore. We are GST-registered and all web prices are inclusive of GST.

Overseas customers (Exports) are not subjected to local taxes. In that case, speak to a sales representive for details. Overseas customers stopping by in singapore for a temporary period of time for a collection can also do a GST refund at the airport when leaving singapore. However, this does not include any possible import duty if the buyer choses international shipping to other countries.

COMZONE will not be responsible for other countries' import duty, though we will endeavour to assist in working with you to clarify things with any required authority. Do contact our sales team if you have any further queries about this.
